Campus Key

Campus Key is a website or web application designed to help students easily find accommodation near their universities. The platform connects students who are searching for housing with verified landlords who have rooms, apartments, or shared spaces available for rent. Students can search for accommodation by filtering options such as price, preferred location, and the university they attend, making it easier to find places that match their needs and budget. The platform then displays suitable listings and allows students to connect directly with landlords or property managers to arrange viewings or secure housing. Overall, Campus Key simplifies the process of finding student accommodation by bringing students and landlords together on one digital platform, saving time and making the search more efficient.
